Planning roadmap
================

The canonical public roadmap remains `ROADMAP.md <https://github.com/apajon/lifecore_ros2/blob/main/ROADMAP.md>`_.

For current sprint execution state, see :doc:`sprints/README`.

After Sprint 13, sprint priority is location-neutral: core, companion,
documentation, architecture, DX, tooling, external modules, and research may all
be valid sprint targets when they reduce risk, improve adoption, clarify
architecture, or unblock strategic sequencing.
